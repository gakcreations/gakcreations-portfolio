# Architectural Details — Abbaye aux Dames

A focused study of the abbey’s intimate architectural language:  
carved capitals, worn arches, sculpted stone, and the quiet textures shaped by centuries of touch, weather, and devotion.

These drawings reveal the abbey not as a monument, but as a living surface —  
a place where time leaves its signature in every groove and shadow.

## 📝 Artist’s Notes
These details were drawn during quiet hours inside the abbey,  
when the light softened and the building revealed its most intimate stories.

Each sketch is a meditation on:

- Craftsmanship  
- Material memory  
- The dialogue between erosion and endurance  
- The human hand in stone
