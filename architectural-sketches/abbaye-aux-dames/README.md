# Abbaye aux Dames — Architectural Sketches

A quiet dialogue between stone, shadow, and centuries.

This collection gathers intimate studies from the Abbaye aux Dames in Saintes, France —
a place where Romanesque geometry softens into human touch, and every carved surface
carries the memory of hands, weather, and devotion. These sketches explore the abbey
not as a monument, but as a living body: textured, worn, luminous, and deeply human.
