# Abbaye aux Dames — Saintes, France

A series of architectural sketches capturing the Romanesque heart of Saintes.  
These drawings explore the abbey’s sculpted stone, rhythmic arches, and the quiet geometry shaped by nearly a thousand years of devotion and human passage.
